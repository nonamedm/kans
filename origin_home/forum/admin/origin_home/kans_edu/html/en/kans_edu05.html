  5. V. Applications of Radiation

    1. Applications of Radiation

      Nowadays, radiation and radioisotopes are widely applied and accepted for the purpose of economic growth, as well as for ensuring that general human health is not compromised. As application technology has its own unique advantages, people develop and utilize this technology continuously while following strict radiation safety regulations. It is extremely important to adhere to safety regulations and standardized protocol to not cause any dangerous situations, while maximizing the benefits of this technology. Typical applications are outlined in the following table.
